Developer Hangout Seeking Work Policy

Developer Hangout does not permit or endorse "looking to hire" or "position wanted" posts

Looking to hire a dev? Or are you trying to pay someone to help you code? Because of how many issues this can cause, we don't allow hiring/payment to be discussed in this server. If you need to hire a dev, Developer Hangout is not the place to look.

Why?

It's very easy to scam online. We simply aren't trying to deal with situations where a user isn't getting paid for their work.

Don't get scammed or used If you see someone posting that they are lookin got hire a dev or willing to pay for help, this should be a red flag. This is a discord community focused around developers looking to better their skills. The majority of our user base are newer devs who are just beginning to learn. Most haven't had a real client before, most are working in the field and most having been paid to code.

Anyone who is trying to hire a developer from this server is looking for cheap help or trying to scam. There are better places to hire a developer. Another huge red flag is if the details can only be discussed in a DM (direct message). This makes it easier for a scammer to be successful, as there won't be anyone else reading the conversation.

Please value your time and don't allow yourself to be used/scammed. You are spending a lot of time, money and resources learning how to become a programmer. This is a valuable skill and there are a ton of people who will take advantage of newer devs. If you ever have any questions or concerns, please feel free to contact a staff member.
